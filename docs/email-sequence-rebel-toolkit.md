## Implementation Notes

### Recommended Platforms
- **ConvertKit** — Best for creators, easy automation
- **Beehiiv** — Great deliverability, newsletter-focused
- **Mailchimp** — Free tier available, widely used

### Timing
- Email 1: Immediately after signup (Day 0)
- Email 2: Day 2
- Email 3: Day 4
- Email 4: Day 6
- Email 5: Day 8

### Personalization
- Replace `{first_name}` with subscriber's name
- Replace `[Your Name]` with your name
- Update testimonials with real customer quotes when available

### Tracking
- Use UTM parameters on all links: `?utm_source=email&utm_campaign=nurture&utm_content=email1`
- Track open rates, click rates, and conversions per email

### A/B Testing Ideas
- Subject lines (curiosity vs. direct)
- Email length (shorter vs. story-driven)
- CTA placement (early vs. end only)
